New Component — Your Fuel (Nutrition)

High-protein nutrition is critical during fertility treatment. This component provides daily food inspiration — not medical advice.

Key design decisions:
• Frame as "inspiration" not prescription — "try this" not "eat this"
• No calorie counts, no portion sizes (not a diet app)
• Rotate through options so it stays fresh
• Show 2-3 simple food ideas per day with protein callout
• Include a small disclaimer
• Dietary preferences collected during onboarding (or in settings)
