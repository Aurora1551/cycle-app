How it works

Day grid: Filled circles = completed days. Bordered circle = current day. Faded = future. Tap any day to revisit it.

Stats: Days done vs total days in the cycle.

Saved: Only appears if you've hearted at least one quote or affirmation. Shows the text, author (for quotes), content type, and which day it was from. Most recent saves at the bottom.

If nothing is saved, the Saved card doesn't show at all — the page just has the grid and stats.

Without saved items

The page would just show the day grid card and the stats card. Clean and simple — the Saved section only appears when you have favorites.
